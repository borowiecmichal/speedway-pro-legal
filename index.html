Privacy Policy — Speedway Pro

Last updated: March 18, 2026

1. Introduction

Speedway Pro ("the App") is a mobile application for managing speedway competitions, developed by mTech Studio ("we", "us", "our"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and applicable Polish law.

2. Data Controller

mTech Michal Borowiec
Contact: borowiecmg@gmail.com

3. Data We Collect

The App collects minimal data:

  • Purchase history: When you subscribe to Speedway Pro Premium, your purchase information (subscription type, purchase date, expiry) is processed by RevenueCat, Inc. to manage your subscription.
  • Anonymous device identifier: A randomly generated identifier used solely to associate your subscription with your device. This is not linked to your personal identity.

4. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Match data, rider names, scores — all stored locally on your device only
  • Location data
  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Photos, contacts, or files
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data

5. How We Use Your Data

The data we collect is used exclusively to:

  • Process and verify your Premium subscription
  • Restore your subscription when you reinstall the App

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party service:

  • RevenueCat, Inc. — subscription management. RevenueCat processes purchase data as a data processor on our behalf. Their privacy policy: revenuecat.com/privacy
  • Google Play Billing — payment processing, governed by Google's Privacy Policy

7. Data Storage and Security

  • All match data is stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences. We have no access to this data.
  • Subscription data is transmitted securely via HTTPS encryption.
  • Uninstalling the App permanently deletes all locally stored data.

8. Your Rights (GDPR)

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access — request what data we hold about you
  • Rectification — request correction of inaccurate data
  • Erasure — request deletion of your data
  • Portability — request a copy of your data
  • Object — object to data processing

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at borowiecmg@gmail.com.

9. Data Retention

Subscription data is retained by RevenueCat for the duration of your subscription and a reasonable period thereafter for billing purposes. Anonymous device identifiers are automatically removed when no longer needed.

10. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
